🦜 Toucan — The Architect of Language
The toucan is responsible for everything related to structure, logic, and how the language fits together. He guides learners through the rules that make communication clear and coherent. From simple grammar to advanced discourse, he shows how to build sentences, connect ideas, and express complex thoughts. His role is to give the learner the “blueprint” of the language.

🐊 Alligator — The Keeper of Words and Knowledge
The alligator handles vocabulary, concepts, and the real-world content of the language. He introduces new words, categories, themes, and topics—from everyday objects to specialized academic terms. His role is to expand the learner’s world, giving them the linguistic tools to talk about anything, at any level.

🌻 Sunflower — The Guide of Expression, Emotion, and Style
The sunflower teaches how to sound natural, expressive, and nuanced. She focuses on adjectives, feelings, tone, style, figurative language, and subtle meaning. As the learner reaches higher levels, she helps refine elegance, precision, and emotional depth in communication. Her role is to shape the learner’s voice and make their language beautiful and meaningful.

🦈 Shark — The Trainer of Communication and Action
The shark is responsible for speaking skills, interaction, and practical communication. He teaches how to use verbs, express actions, hold conversations, tell stories, and navigate social situations. As the learner advances, he guides them through argumentation, persuasion, and fluent, confident speech. His role is to make the learner an effective communicator.
